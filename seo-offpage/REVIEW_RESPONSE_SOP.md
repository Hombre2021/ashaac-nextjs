# Review Response SOP (Local SEO + Reputation)

## Objectives

- Increase trust and conversion rate.
- Improve local ranking signals through consistent customer interaction.
- Resolve negative experiences quickly and professionally.

## SLA

- Positive review: respond within 24–48 hours.
- Neutral/negative review: respond within 12–24 hours.

## Tone rules

- Be respectful and specific.
- Thank the reviewer by first name when possible.
- Mention service context naturally (city/service type) without keyword stuffing.
- Never disclose private customer details.
- Offer direct resolution path for complaints.

## Positive review template

Hi [First Name], thank you for trusting All Solutions Heating and Air Conditioning for your [service type] in [city]. We appreciate your feedback and are glad we could help improve your comfort. If you ever need support, call or text us at 801-755-3040.

## Neutral review template

Hi [First Name], thank you for your feedback. We appreciate the opportunity to serve you and we are always working to improve. If there is anything we can do to make this right, please contact us directly at 801-755-3040 and we will be happy to help.

## Negative review template

Hi [First Name], thank you for bringing this to our attention. We are sorry to hear about your experience and would like to make this right immediately. Please call or text us at 801-755-3040 and ask for [Manager Name] so we can review the details and resolve this as quickly as possible.

## Escalation policy

Escalate before posting if review includes:

- Safety incident
- Billing dispute with legal risk
- Threats/harassment
- Claims of property damage

## Review request SMS template

Hi [First Name], thank you again for choosing All Solutions Heating and Air Conditioning today. If you have 30 seconds, would you share your experience in a Google review? It helps local families find trusted HVAC service: [GBP Review Link]

## Follow-up SMS (72 hours)

Hi [First Name], quick follow-up from All Solutions HVAC. If your system is running well, we’d appreciate your feedback here: [GBP Review Link]

## Final reminder (7 days)

Hi [First Name], thank you again for your business. If you’d still like to leave a review, here is the link: [GBP Review Link]
