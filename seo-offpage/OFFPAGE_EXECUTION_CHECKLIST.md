# Off-Page SEO Execution Checklist (Publish-Ready)

Use this checklist to improve local rankings for All Solutions Heating and Air Conditioning after technical/on-page SEO is already in place.

## 0) Setup (one-time)

- [ ] Confirm canonical NAP is identical everywhere:
  - Business name: All Solutions Heating and Air Conditioning LLC
  - Address: 4434 W 8790 S, West Jordan, UT 84088
  - Phone: 801-755-3040 (primary), 801-512-7103 (secondary)
  - Website: https://ashaac.com
- [ ] Confirm service area wording across listings: West Jordan + Salt Lake County cities.
- [ ] Assign ownership:
  - Owner (approvals)
  - Executor (publishes, outreach, citations)
  - Reviewer (quality + consistency check)
- [ ] Create a monthly KPI sheet tracking:
  - Calls from Google Business Profile
  - Website clicks from GBP
  - Direction requests
  - New reviews and average rating
  - New referring domains
  - Top city + service keyword movement

## 1) Weekly GBP cadence

### Posting frequency

- [ ] 2 GBP posts per week (minimum)
  - Post 1: service/education post (before/after, maintenance tip, system type)
  - Post 2: offer/CTA post (estimate, financing, seasonal reminder)

### Weekly GBP checklist

- [ ] Add 2–4 fresh photos per week (job site, equipment, team, before/after).
- [ ] Answer all new Q&A items within 24 hours.
- [ ] Respond to all new reviews within 24–48 hours.
- [ ] Update service categories if needed (primary + secondary relevance check).
- [ ] Verify service areas/cities are current.

### GBP post template

- **Title:** HVAC [Service Type] in [City]
- **Body template:**
  - We completed a [service type] in [city] this week.
  - Outcome: [comfort/efficiency/reliability result].
  - If you need [service], request a free estimate today.
- **CTA:** Call now / Learn more / Book
- **Link target:** Relevant page on https://ashaac.com (service page or city page)

## 3) Review acquisition + response SOP

### Review acquisition workflow (every completed job)

- [ ] Send review request SMS within 2 hours of job completion.
- [ ] Send follow-up reminder after 72 hours if no response.
- [ ] Send final reminder after 7 days.
- [ ] Ask customer to mention city + service type naturally.

### Response SLA

- [ ] Positive reviews: respond within 24–48 hours.
- [ ] Neutral/negative reviews: initial response within 12–24 hours.
- [ ] Escalate any safety/legal issues internally before posting detailed reply.

## 4) Backlink execution cadence

### Weekly cadence

- [ ] 20 personalized outreach emails/week.
- [ ] 5 follow-ups/week (for prior non-responders).
- [ ] 1 local PR/newsworthy angle/month.

### Target backlink sources

- [ ] Local businesses (cross-referrals)
- [ ] Property managers / real estate teams
- [ ] Local bloggers/newsletters
- [ ] Local nonprofit sponsorship pages
- [ ] Chamber and association member pages
- [ ] Supplier/manufacturer partner pages

## 5) Monthly reporting checklist

- [ ] Capture GSC impressions/clicks for city + service queries.
- [ ] Capture GBP metrics (calls, clicks, direction requests).
- [ ] Log new referring domains and live links.
- [ ] Log net new reviews + average rating change.
- [ ] Identify top 3 winning pages and top 3 pages needing support.
- [ ] Define next month’s city/service focus list.

## 6) Quality guardrails (must-follow)

- [ ] No fake reviews or incentivized review language.
- [ ] No spammy bulk directory blasts.
- [ ] No exact-match anchor over-optimization.
- [ ] Keep NAP consistent across every profile.
- [ ] Keep outreach personalized and relevant.

## Recommended weekly workflow (60–90 minutes)

1. GBP: publish 2 posts, upload photos, respond to reviews/Q&A.
2. Citations: submit/update 3–5 listings.
3. Backlinks: send 20 outreach emails + 5 follow-ups.
4. Tracking: update KPI and attribution notes.
