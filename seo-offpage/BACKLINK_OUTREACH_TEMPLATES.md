# Backlink Outreach Templates

## Outreach cadence

- New outreach: 20 contacts per week
- Follow-up 1: 4 business days later
- Follow-up 2: 7 business days after first follow-up

## Prospect categories

- Local real estate teams
- Property managers
- Home service partners (plumbers, electricians, roofers)
- Local blogs/newsletters
- Chambers/associations
- Sponsor pages and community organizations

## Anchor text guidance

Use varied, natural anchors:

- branded: All Solutions Heating and Air Conditioning
- partial service anchors: HVAC installation in West Jordan
- generic anchors: local HVAC team, learn more, this guide

Avoid repetitive exact-match anchors across many links.
